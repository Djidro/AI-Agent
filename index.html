Why it works

Built for people who don't have time to scroll job boards

One setup, then your agent does the repetitive part: searching, scoring, filtering, and watching for scams — across every major Gulf source.

⚡

Works while you're offline

The agent runs on a schedule in the background. You don't need the app open, your phone on, or your laptop awake — it messages you on Telegram the moment a match appears.

🎯

AI Match Score on every job

Every listing is scored 0–100 against your country, role, salary, visa and accommodation preferences, so you only act on the ones actually worth your time.

🛡️

Scam & duplicate filtering

Suspicious listings — missing company details, unrealistic pay, sketchy links — get flagged automatically. Duplicate postings are silently dropped.

🛂

Visa sponsorship detection

The agent reads job descriptions for visa sponsorship, work permits and relocation language, and highlights it clearly so you don't have to dig.

📊

Daily reports

A short Telegram summary each day: total jobs found, high matches, visa-sponsored roles, and the single best opportunity available right now.

🆓

Completely free to run

Hosted on GitHub Pages, automated with GitHub Actions, delivered through Telegram. No servers, no subscriptions, no hidden costs.

How it works

Three steps, then it runs itself

There's a real order here — each step has to happen before the next one makes sense.

1

Create your agent

Tell it your target countries, roles, minimum salary, and whether you need visa sponsorship or accommodation. Takes about two minutes.

2

It scans continuously

A scheduled job searches hospitality listings across the Gulf, scores each one against your profile, checks for scams and duplicates.

3

You get pinged on Telegram

Only jobs that clear your match threshold land in your chat — with the score, the visa status, and a direct link to apply.
